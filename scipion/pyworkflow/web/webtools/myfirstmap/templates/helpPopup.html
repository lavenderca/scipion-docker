Step 1: Upload and import your files to the web portal clicking on the graph on the left hand side of this panel, on the Import Averages box.



Finally press "Execute"!


Step 2: Select a method to obtain your initial map . You can choose among Xmipp - RANSAC, EMAN initial volume and Xmipp - Significant.



Parameters can be easily user customized. Three level of usages are supported (Normal, Advanced and Expert), which should be chosen according to your experience with each of the methods



Finally press "Execute"!


Step 3: Once a method has finished processing, clicking on its box shows at the bottom a summary of Inputs and Outputs.



Further analyze of the results of each method can be achieved by first clicking on its corresponding box and then clicking on the Analyze Results button.




Step 4: The outputs of several methods can be mutually aligned in 3D and presented to the user for further selection using the align volume box. Note that you should select one of the output 3D volumes to be used as 3D reference.



Finally press "Execute"!


Step 5: Aligned volumes can be downloaded using the corresponding button .
