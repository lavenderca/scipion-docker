Note that this Web Portal may run in two configurations: Guest/Demo and as an Instruct Access Project. In a Guest/Demo configuration, we limit the size of your total set of uploaded movies to 25 GigaBytes (this mode is intended to provide just with a quick look on the Web Portal capabilities). As an Instruct Access Project, I2PC staff will have already take care of the correct uploading of your entire project. To demo the maximum size to storage in the upload space is 25 gigabytes.



Step 2: Import your files to the Web Portal clicking on the graph on the left hand side of this panel, on the Import Movies box.



Finally press "Execute"!


Step 3: Select the method Xmipp - Movie Alignment.



Parameters can be easily user customized. Two level of usages are supported (Normal and Advanced), which should be chosen according to your experience. The difference is the set of parameters you are allowed to change



Finally press "Execute"!


Step 4: Once the method has finished processing, clicking on its box shows a summary of Inputs and Outputs at the bottom.



Further analyze of the results of the method can be achieved by first clicking on its corresponding box and then clicking on the Analyze Results button.




Step 5: The corrected averaged micrographs can be downloaded using the corresponding button .
