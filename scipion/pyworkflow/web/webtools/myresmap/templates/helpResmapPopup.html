Step 1: Upload and import your files to the web portal clicking on the graph on the left hand side of this panel, on the Import Volumes box.



Once uploaded, press "Execute"!


Step 2: Select the method resmap - local resolution.



Parameters can be easily user customized. Two level of usages are supported (Normal and Advanced), which should be chosen according to your experience.



Finally press "Execute"!


Methods results can be further analyzed by first clicking on the corresponding Method box and then clicking on the Analyze Results button.
